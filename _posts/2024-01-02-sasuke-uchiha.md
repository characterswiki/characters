Sasuke Uchiha is the last surviving member of the Uchiha clan after the massacre orchestrated by his older brother, Itachi. He seeks revenge and later redemption, mastering powerful techniques such as the Sharingan and Rinnegan. He becomes a wandering shinobi and plays a pivotal role in protecting the village while pursuing his own path.
