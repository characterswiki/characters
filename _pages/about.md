## About Us

Anime Manga Characters Wiki is a dedicated encyclopedia focused on detailed profiles of anime and manga characters.

We cover:

- Character biographies
- Powers & abilities
- Affiliations
- Story arcs
- Origins
- Transformations

Our goal is to provide accurate and structured character information for fans worldwide.
